# Year 1 — Prove the Architecture (Independent Path)

## What We Want To Achieve This Year

Climb **Rungs 1-2** of the capability ladder and secure a path to the data needed for Rung 3.
Do it solo, with data we can access today, depending on no single institution.

- **Rung 1 — Strong retinal representation** (public data: EyePACS, Messidor, FGADR)
- **Rung 2 — Temporal modeling on real sequences** (OLIVES — the first Synapse-shaped data)

We do NOT claim Rung 3+ until controlled data and evidence support it.

## The Year 1 North Star Metric

By Month 12: a preprint demonstrating (a) strong retinal representation learning and
(b) a functioning temporal dynamics prototype on OLIVES including treatment information,
PLUS at least one controlled-data pathway approved or in late-stage review.

## The Honest Year 1 Claim Boundary

- WE CAN SAY: "An open-data temporal prototype showing multimodal retinal disease
  states can be modeled over time, including treatment information."
- WE CANNOT SAY: "We solved / validated diabetic retinopathy progression at scale."

See docs/CLAIMS.md for the live, current claim boundary.

## The Two Tracks

**Track 1 — Build now (solo, Months 1-6):**
OLIVES feasibility audit → per-eye sequences → RETFound encoding → baselines →
latent dynamics prototype → treatment-conditioning → preprint.

**Track 2 — Secure scale data (parallel, Months 1-18):**
UK Biobank application (Month 1) · EyePACS partnership inquiry (Month 2-3) ·
BioLINCC ACCORD request (Month 3) · local retina-clinic pilot outreach (Month 4-6).
Multiple independent routes — no single dependency.

## The UW Posture

If a UW affiliation materializes (MSECE, Jan 2027), treat it as a bonus accelerant for
IRB, UW Medicine collaboration, and co-authorship. Build relationships opportunistically.
NEVER gate a milestone, sprint, or funding decision on it.

## Year 1 Gates

- **Gate 1 (Month 3):** Infra + baselines work + OLIVES audited?
- **Gate 2 (Month 6):** Architecture functions on real temporal data?
- **Gate 3 (Month 12):** Earned the right to pursue seriously?

Each gate: CONTINUE / ITERATE / PIVOT. See docs/MILESTONES.md for criteria.
The willingness to pivot or fold at a failed gate is what separates discipline from delusion.

## Runway Reality

Near-zero burn through Month 6 (<$500). All Year 1 data is free (OLIVES, EyePACS,
Messidor, FGADR). First real cash need (UK Biobank ~£9K) is timed to coincide with
SBIR/grant funding, not personal runway. Keep income stable; quit nothing.
